Program model

How the initiative works.

Start with one excellent talk. Build a repeatable workshop. Document impact. Expand into a team. Host events. Grow the record over time.

Step 01

School talk

Deliver a crisp presentation on assistive technology, accessibility, and inclusive problem-solving.

Step 02

Design workshop

Guide a short design sprint: students sketch assistive-tech solutions for real accessibility barriers.

Step 03

Idea archive

Collect student concepts, reflections, and outcomes to build a visible record of impact over time.

Step 04

Scale outward

Expand into a student team, more schools, and an annual assistive-tech design challenge.
