About Us

What is AssistiveTech Bridge?

AssistiveTech Bridge is a student-led nonprofit initiative dedicated to closing the gap between emerging assistive technologies and the next generation of creators and problem-solvers.

We believe every student — regardless of ability — deserves to understand how technology can be a bridge to opportunity, independence, and full participation in the world.

Sanjey Gopinath

Sanjey Gopinath founded AssistiveTech Bridge with a clear conviction: that understanding accessibility should not wait until college or the workforce.

Through school talks, workshops, and hands-on sessions, Sanjey is building a movement of young inclusive innovators, one classroom at a time.
