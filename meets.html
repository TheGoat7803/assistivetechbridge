Format

What to expect at a session.

Every school visit follows the same core format so it is easy to request, easy to host, and easy to repeat.

20 min

Core talk

What is assistive technology? Why does it matter? Real examples, real impact, real stories.

10 min

Q&A discussion

Students ask questions. Sanjey shares context on bioengineering, engineering careers, and accessibility design.

15 min

Design sprint

Small groups sketch a solution to a real accessibility barrier. Paper, pen, or digital — any format works.

5 min

Wrap-up

Groups share ideas. Sanjey gives brief feedback. Students leave with a clearer sense of what inclusive design means.
